Duelists

Black Panther

  • A.K.A. "A More Useful Iron Fist"
  • Punches the shit out of you and kicks too.
  • Fast as fuck. Rarely played.

Black Widow

  • A.K.A. "What if Snipers Didn't Kill?"
  • Should be better than she is. Trash bullets.
  • Ultimate seems half useless.

Hawkeye

  • A.K.A. "Annoying Ass Bow Guy"
  • Hits hard and hits fast. Annoying to deal with but not that useful unless someone's a GOD.
  • You're a bit of a tool if you play him..

Hela

  • A.K.A. "Head Tapper"
  • Genuinely good if you can aim. Not played that much though.
  • Very strong ultimate ability.

The Human Torch

  • A.K.A. "Flamer"
  • Less effective version of Iron Man basically.
  • Why would you choose him?

Iron Fist

  • A.K.A. "Johnny Sins"
  • Fast punches. Super annoying to deal with. Not that helpful for a team, though.
  • You're a tool if you use him.

Iron Man

  • A.K.A. "Steel Sissy"
  • Flies around and blasts you. Annoying to deal with.
  • You have to pick a character to counter him or you'll have a long day.

Magik

  • A.K.A. "Sword Bitch"
  • Swings a sword. Kind of teleports. Decent ultimate.
  • Rarely played for a reason. Fairly ineffective.

Mr. Fantastic

  • A.K.A. "Stretch Armstrong"
  • Almost impossible to kill. Possibly the most annoying character in the game.
  • Possibly should be a tank rather than a DPS.

Moon Knight

  • A.K.A. "Ethan's Crush"
  • "THE MOON IS HAUNTING YOU"
  • Drops annoying oncs. Bounces his shots. Stupid ult. Broken dumbass character.

Namor

  • A.K.A. "Not Aqua Man
  • Annoying ass squid/octopus turrets to deal with.
  • Literally throws a fucking whale at you. Literally. A fucking whale.

Psylocke

  • A.K.A. "Purple Haired Bitch"
  • Looks like she's going to cut you with swords but she actually shoots you.
  • Ultimate is hard to deal with but also not that great when you have it.

Scarlet Witch

  • A.K.A. "Wanda Vision"
  • Basically has auto aim.
  • Floats around and hits. Ultimate is ridiculous.

Spiderman

  • A.K.A. "The Twink of New York"
  • If you use him, you're either fourteen years old or a moron...or both.
  • Does some damage but ultimately doesn't help his team at all.

Squirrel Girl

  • A.K.A. "Nutter Butter"
  • The thickest and possibly most annoying character to play against.
  • Her rounds bounce off walls and her ultimate is incredibly frustrating.

Star Lord

  • A.K.A. "Chris Pratt"
  • Floats around and shoots his little mini guns. Punisher if he kind of flew.
  • Loves to yell shit in your ear.

Storm

  • A.K.A. "Salt 'n' Pepper"
  • Flies around and hits you with tornadoes and shit.
  • Can be really good but most people are trash with her. High skill ceiling.

The Punisher

  • A.K.A. "Dredd"
  • Your friends will accuse you of "sitting on your little turret" every time you use his full kit.
  • Can rip if you know how to actually use him.

Winter Soldier

  • A.K.A. "Captain's Twink"
  • Yells "I'M GAY" a lot because his father never accepted him for who he was.
  • Deeply repressed. His hook is annoying as well.

Wolverine

  • A.K.A. "Hugh"
  • Shoves up on you and slashes you in the face.
  • A good one is very fucking difficult to stop. A mediocre one is useless.
