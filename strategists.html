Adam Warlock

Cloak and Dagger

  • A.K.A. "The Woke Agenda"

Invisible Woman

  • A.K.A. "Tits McGee"
  • Can make shit invisible.
  • Drops healing bubbles that you'll never see if David doesn't yell at you.

Jeff the Land Shark

  • A.K.A. "Shark Boy No Lava Girl"
  • Annoying as shit to try and kill but mostly useless.
  • Dumb ass ultimate but mostly just a meme.

Loki

  • A.K.A. "Clone Candyman"
  • Can make clones of himself which is super annoying.
  • Can copy your character and then use your ultimate. It's stupid.

Luna Snow

  • A.K.A. "A DJ Who Actually Does Something Useful"
  • Can freeze you and heal the shit out of her teammates.
  • Hits harder than you think. Ultimate is pretty sick and lasts a while.

Mantis

  • A.K.A. "Chris Pratt's Love Interest"
  • Heals, hits, stuns and kicks a whole lot of ass.
  • A lot to micro-manage when you play her.

Rocket Raccoon

  • A.K.A. "Rocky Raccoon"
  • Can jet around and heal the shit out of people.
  • Useless ass mini gun.
